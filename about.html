Behind The Creations
My name is Veronica, I’m the owner and maker of Camilo Creations!

My 9 to 5 is spent in a creative environment, where I happily design sleepwear for the industry in which I pursued when I graduated from FIT in 2014. All these years in fashion design have exposed me and expanded my knowledge into graphics, packaging, marketing and more- all of which I apply when I design, package and deliver Camilo Creations.

Starting out by word of mouth and Instagram, I finally decided to take the leap and join the Etsy community in hopes to share my creations with the world. I hope you guys enjoy my designs and enjoy the experience that Camilo Creations has to offer. From the services I provide, down to the packaging – I aim for the best qualities, materials, and more.

Thanks to my clients’ special requests and collaboration, I’ve been able to offer personalized products in mugs, wine glasses, make up bags, baby onesies, bachelorette party & wedding gear, unique part decor and more! Please follow on social media @camilocreations to join the experience!
